Challenges in ERP Transformations

Implementation Costs

Initial costs can spiral without proper cost controls and scope management. Design to Cost helps ensure a disciplined approach.

Complex Integrations

Managing complex integrations across business units requires a careful balance of cost and functionality.

Legacy Systems

The challenge of transitioning from legacy systems to new platforms can cause unforeseen expenses.

User Adoption

Ensuring user adoption and minimizing training costs are critical to achieving cost efficiency.

Solutions for Cost Efficiency

Design to Cost Methodology

Implement a "Design to Cost" approach to optimize processes, limit unnecessary customizations, and ensure alignment with cost-saving goals.

Modular Implementations

Roll out modular implementations to avoid overwhelming the organization and distribute costs over phases.

Process Standardization

Standardize business processes to limit costs related to complexity and customization during ERP rollouts.

Optimized Change Management

Reduce costs associated with user adoption by focusing on training and change management early in the process.
